Payroll Services

We offer weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly payroll assistance at whatever level you may need. We will provide you with accurate federal, state and local tax calculations for each pay period, and provide a detailed statement that shows pay and deductions for each employee. With this assistance, the employer will retain the responsibility of writing payroll checks as at when needed.

﻿

﻿

We can also provide full service payroll support which includes:

• Guaranteed accurate federal, state and local tax calculations

• Direct deposit

• Professional paychecks and pay stubs

• Secure online employee access to pay stubs

• Payments to independent contractors

• Wage garnishments

• Vacation and sick pay tracking

• Pay types: salary, hourly wages, multiple pay rates, bonuses, cash tips, paycheck tips, expense reimbursements, allowances, holiday, etc.

• Voluntary deductions: taxable and pretax premiums for medical, dental, and vision; retirement plans; flexible spending accounts: dependent and medical care; cash advance and loan repayments; etc.

• Employer reports: Tax Liability, Tax and Wage Summary, Total Pay, Tax Payments, Workers’ Compensation, Vacation and Sick Leave, Billing Summary, Total Cost, Retirement Plans

• Employee reports: Payroll Summary, Payroll Details, Deductions, Last Paycheck, Employee Details
